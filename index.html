My Passions

Technology

From a young age, I have been fascinated by technology and all the wonderful things it can do. At the age of 12, I built my first PC - a huge accomplishment for me at the time - and I haven't looked back since. Today, I work on projects of all sorts as I look to build my career in the computing field (check out my projects for more).

To me, technology is powerful because it connects us and motivates us. Our wonderful smart devices allow us to connect with our loved ones in ways that were previously unthinkable. Perhaps more incredible is how we are able to use technology to make the world a better place. From space exploration to the smartphone to electric vehicles, human innovation is truly limitless, and I can't wait to see what the future holds.

Travel and Adventure

My life bucket list is pretty much to travel the world and visit as many places as I can. I can thank my parents for my love of travelling, as every year they plan a trip to a different place and bring me along for the ride. I love experiencing new things, and travelling allows me to do just that. Different and unique landscapes, cultures, foods, and people - that's what it's all about. I'm proud to say I have set foot in all of the provinces and territories in the beautiful country of Canada, except one. I hope to continue the travel the Earth (and perhaps beyond) and experience the many wonders that the world has to offer.

What I love most about travelling is the thrill of adventure, and one sport that gives that same thrill is snowboarding. The excitement of being barelling down the hill with snow spraying in your face is second to none. I love snowboarding so much that I earned a snowboard instructor qualification. However, I have only been able to hit the slopes in Canada, and while Canadian hills are nothing to scoff at, but they definitely don't compare to the real mountains like the Alps, which I hope one day I will conquer.

Food

Being a small, skinny guy, it probably doesn't seem like I eat a lot - but I love food. Specifically, I like trying new, different foods. Don't get me wrong though, I'm not one of those picky food connoisseurs. I love amazing food, but I'll settle for anything that tastes good, even if it's short of perfect. It doesn't have to be fancy or expensive - some Kraft Dinner gets the job done when you're in a pinch!

Oh, and I'll eat pretty much anything. Something may seem nasty to you, but to me, it's just food! As long as it tastes good and won't kill me, I'm cool with it! I could never settle for one favourite food, but if I had to pick a category, it would be seafood. I never really appreciated seafood until I tried fresh lobster from the Atlantic. Believe me, the freshness makes all the difference, and there's no going back once you've tried it.
